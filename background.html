Arima is a font family that has 7 different line weights;
Thin, Extra Light, Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold and Bold.
It has elements of calligraphy and utilizes soft edges to give a flowy look.


The goal of it is to appeal to both casual and professional designers.

developed by one person but is a project led by NDISCOVER which

to work well as a web font in the mobile era.
